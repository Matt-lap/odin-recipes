The Perfect Steak

The Easiest Method For Cooking a Steak on The Stovetop

In only 15 minutes you can have the very best stovetop steak of your life!

You can make it with only 4 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Ribeye cut steak at least 1-inch thick
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 Cloves smashed garlic
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs

Instructions

  1. Prep The Steaks Remove the steaks from the refrigerator about 30 minutes to 1 hour before cooking. Place them on a paper towel-lined plate and pat dry with more paper towels. This will help dry the surface of the meat, making a better crust.
